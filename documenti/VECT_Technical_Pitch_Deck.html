How VECT Operates

Zero-Coordination

A single wideband carrier handles the entire production. Frequency coordination errors and intermodulation products are mathematically eliminated.

I/O Mapping

Native 32x32 Dante infrastructure with parallel MADI redundancy. Integrates seamlessly with Calrec, DiGiCo, and Yamaha setups in seconds.

SEK Hardware

Bodypacks capable of simultaneous IEM output and Mic/Line input. One pack per talent handles all communication and monitoring needs with zero weight penalty.

Zone Design

Implementation of multizone Spectera antenna networks (DAD) for large-scale festivals, ensuring zero dropouts even in the most congested RF environments.

Transmission Philosophy

Spectera utilizes sophisticated TDMA/TDD multicarrier protocols. This generates a continuous bidirectional uplink and downlink between the Base Station and all connected SEK bodypacks simultaneously.

11 selectable Audio Link Modes (ALM) allow granular optimization of audio quality versus transmission range based on the mission requirements.
